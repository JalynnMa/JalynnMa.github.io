Fan Chengcheng

Fan Chengcheng (Chinese: 范丞丞; born 16 June 2000) is a Chinese singer and actor. He was a member of the Chinese boy group, Nine Percent debuted on 6 April 2018, and is currently a member of Yuehua's NEX7.

The story start at...

In the summer of 2018,Fan Chengcheng and other 108 boys join the Televiosion show Idol Producer,which is aimed at choosing 9 boys to make up a team.At first, fan Chengcheng's singing and dancing ability was not outstanding.

At the beginning of the placement examination, he only got the C grade. However, in the public stage after that, fan Chengcheng showed his original ability with his own efforts and won the favor of many audiences.

In the final group night competition, fan Chengcheng finally became a member of nine percent group with a good result of third place.
